Smart Pressure e-Mat

A non-invasive, privacy-preserving AI system designed to classify human sleep postures in real-time using Velostat piezoresistive sensors and a state-of-the-art CRNN architecture.

Launch Live Dashboard

How It Works

The Hardware: Velostat Matrix

The system utilizes a 27x27 sensor grid made from Velostat, a unique piezoresistive material. When a patient lies on the mat, their body weight compresses the material, instantly changing its electrical resistance. This allows us to capture highly accurate, continuous "pressure images" without ever needing a camera in the room.

The Brain: ResNet & LSTM

The raw sensor data is messy and complex. We pass 10-frame sequential tensors into our customized Deep Learning architecture. The ResNet-PI (CNN) extracts the spatial shape of the body, while the LSTM memory network analyzes how the posture changes over time, yielding >99% classification accuracy.

The 4 Posture Classes

Our neural network is currently trained to identify four primary sleeping configurations.

Supine (On Back)

Broad pressure distribution across the shoulder blades, lower back, and heels. Considered the baseline resting position.

Prone (On Stomach)

Pressure concentrated on the chest, knees, and toes. Important to monitor as it can restrict airways in vulnerable patients.

Left Lateral

Narrow, highly concentrated pressure profile on the left shoulder and hip. Vital for preventing localized tissue damage.

Right Lateral

Mirror of the left lateral. Used in medical rotation schedules to ensure even blood flow throughout the night.

Clinical Applications

🛑 Bedsore Prevention

Automatically alerts nurses if a paralyzed or comatose patient has been in the same posture for more than 2 hours.

📉 Fall Detection

Detects rapid shifts in the center of mass toward the edge of the mat, warning caregivers before an elderly patient falls.

🧠 Seizure Monitoring

The LSTM network tracks chaotic, high-frequency pressure spikes to alert staff of potential epileptic episodes.

🔒 Absolute Privacy

Because the AI only sees "pressure blobs," it provides 24/7 medical monitoring without violating patient dignity with cameras.
